# Project Planning Mind Map

A practical work-breakdown mind map for project execution.

## Example

```plantuml
@startmindmap
* Q3 Launch Plan
** Discovery
*** User Interviews
*** Problem Framing
*** Requirements
** Delivery
*** Backend APIs
*** Frontend UI
*** QA Automation
** Metrics
*** Activation Rate
*** Retention D7
*** Error Budget

left side
** Risks
*** Scope Creep
*** Hiring Delay
*** Vendor Dependency
** Mitigations
*** Cut Nice-to-have
*** Weekly Review
*** Rollout Plan
@endmindmap
```

## Pattern Notes

1. Keep planning maps action-oriented and measurable.
2. Put risks/mitigations on one side for quick executive review.
3. Combine this with style classes when presenting to stakeholders.
